We Have The best Research Center!

Healthcare at Ramaiah Memorial hospital is not just about treating ailments, but a haven for spreading compassion, warmth, congeniality and care. With an indomitable team of doctors and experts whose proficiency is backed by cutting-edge equipment and techno-sharp amenities, we constantly strive to upgrade ourselves to the latest technology and developments in medical care to provide unmatched service to our patients.

RMC is involved in undertaking various clinical and drug trials in its Clinical Research. The Lab takes on clinical trials for Indian as well as international pharmaceutical companies. It has been a site for several FDA and ERA studies.Research conducted under strict guidelines has resulted in the College being recognised by several auditory bodies such as EMEA and USFDA. The College has initiated research in areas such as Orthopaedics, Endocrinology, Cardiology and Gastroenterology among others.

Various neurological disorders that are handled by the department are – stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease and other movement disorders, demyelinating disorders such as multiple sclerosis, neurodegenerative disorders such as motor neuron disease and Alzeimers, neuro infections, headache, vertigo, auto-immune disorders, neuropathies, muscle disorders and myasthenia gravis.

  • Speciality clinics treat patients with stroke, epilepsy, movement disorders, demyelinating disorders, and cognitive neurology
  • The department has an associated state‐of‐the‐art clinical neurophysiology laboratory with performs EEG, EMG, nerve conduction and evoked potentials studies
  • Offers expert consultation on a range of sub-specialities and provides holistic treatment services
